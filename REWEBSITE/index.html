SKILL DEVELOPMENT CENTER

The Automotive Skill Development Training programme is a 3 month job oriented residential programme. This is a joint initiative by the Eicher Group Foundation (Eicher motors Ltd. & Royal Enfield) and Amal Jyothi College of Engineering, delivering to the young aspiring students as totally free of cost. After the successful completion of this course, we will provide placement to the students.

Admissions for Automotive Skill Development Training Programme Start's Soon

FEATURES

  • The course is entirey free of cost, including tution fee, study materials, uniforms, accommodation etc.
  • Certificate is issued by Eicher Group of Companies
  • The skill development residential training programme is for 3 months including on-site job experience
  • Placement opportunities will be provided to all eligible student candidates
  • Certificates will be provided by National Skill Development Corporation [NSDC]

SUITABLE CANDIDATES

  • Diploma graduates, IT| graduates, Engineering aspirants and those who passed 10th standard.
